Authors' Page

Instructions for Authors

Every article shall carry a take-home message for practical action towards upliftment of Indian Knowledge Systems and humanity at large
Further, every paper shall have message to common man / non traditional practitioners/professionals
Hand written final manuscripts are accepted for publication. The same shall be as per the legibility conditions given below
  • The hand written manuscripts shall be written in black pens
  • The writing shall be legible with no issues on readability
  • Photos and figures should be pasted in pdf file using appropriate software
All finally accepted papers shall be submitted in a camera-ready format
All local language papers shall have elongated abstracts in English
The paper format/template can be downloaded from the following link
Link to Download the paper format

Editorial

Research based travelogues, reports and case studies
Review Papers
Research questions
Recommendations, reviews and opinions

Plagiarism

The plagiarism percentage accepted is a maximum of 20% for review papers and 15% for other kinds of papers.
The authors shall take written permission from applicable sources for the use of their material in the paper, and the same shall be uploaded during submission.

Copyright transfer

This is a Diamond open access Journal (Free for readers - and - Free for authors)
The journal reserves copyright of the publications
Appropriate declaration in this regards has to be signed in the format available on the website

Reviewing

Reviewing is blinded and would be done both by domain experts and non-domain enthusiasts. Decision of the review committee will be final.

Publication Charges/Payment

There shall be no publication charges.
To promote researchers and research in the area of Indian Knowledge Systems, a token of appreciation shall be paid as honorarium to the communicating author; the sum of which would be decided at the time of publication. Hence, Pūrṇam is a 'Paying' Journal.

Paper Writing Assistance

In view of promoting propagation of IKS, the journal has willingness to provide need-based free assistance in paper writing, subject to availability of volunteers in the language:
  • For individuals having good technical/professional content, but are illiterate and hence need writing assistance
  • For traditional practitioners who have good practical experience or work content and proper evidence, but are unable to put it on paper
  • For individuals who are able to write, but not having a professional touch; wherein assistance will be provided to write the colloquial content in a professional manner
In the above context, the journal invites volunteers who can take up the said tasks. Further, the journal requests intimation about such potential/worthiness of publication of work. Unpublicized inputs in audio form are also considered for potential publication. Needless to say, such prepared papers are also subject to blinded peer review before acceptance.
