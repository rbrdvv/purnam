Instructions for Authors

Every article shall carry a take-home message for practical action towards upliftment of Indian Knowledge Systems and humanity at large
Further, every paper shall have message to common man / non traditional practitioners/professionals
Hand written final manuscripts are accepted for publication. The same shall be as per the legibility conditions given below
  • The hand written manuscripts shall be written in black pens
  • The writing shall be legible with no issues on readability
  • Photos and figures should be pasted in pdf file using appropriate software
All finally accepted papers shall be submitted in a camera-ready format
Local language papers: All local language papers shall have elongated abstracts in English
Paper format/template. The paper format/template can be downloaded from the journal website

Journal contents / Types of articles

Editorial
Letters to Editor
Invited articles
Original research articles
Research based travelogues, reports and case studies
Review articles
Research questions
Recommendations, reviews and opinions

Journal contents / Types of articles

The plagiarism percentage accepted is maximum of 20% for review papers and 15% for other kinds of papers
The authors shall take written permission from applicable sources for use of their material in the paper, and the same shall be uploaded during submission
